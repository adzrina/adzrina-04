My work 

    Over the years, photography has gone through significant evolution, becoming a fascinating and diverse kind of visual art. Using digital sensors or materials sensitive to light, it is the process of taking and producing photographs. "Photography" originates from the Greek terms "graphos," which means drawing, and "photos," which means light. This means that you are drawing with light, which is a fantastic way to capture the spirit of the medium.

"Every photograph is a story captured in a fraction of a second."
